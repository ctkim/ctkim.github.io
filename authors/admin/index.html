CHRISTINE KIM

was born in a small town in South Korea and moved to NYC when she was 9 years old. Ever since she got her hands on her mom’s old camcorder, she made videos and acted out her imaginary stories. She graduated from Brandeis University in 2017 with a B.A. in film (with a B.S. in Computer Science), and has lived in Queens ever since.

She loves telling stories in any creative capacity. She is especially passionate about discovering the world through the eyes of a working class immigrant and highlighting the queer Asian American experience.

Currently, she splits her time between acting, writing code, and trying to revive her sourdough starter.
