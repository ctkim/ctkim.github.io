CHRISTINE EUNJI KIM

was born in South Korea and moved to NYC when she was 9 years old. Ever since she got her hands on her mom’s old camcorder, she acted in her own short films with her uncooperative brother. She graduated from Brandeis University with degrees in Film & CS and has lived in Queens ever since.

Christine loves telling stories in any creative capacity. She is especially passionate about showing the world through the lens of a working class immigrant and highlighting the queer Asian American experience.

Currently, she splits her time between acting, producing, and working towards a Black Belt in Taekwondo.
